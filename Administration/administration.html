I, as a President of Gayatri Vidya Parishad and Chairman, Governing Body of Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering for Women feel proud of introducing this College to the prospective professionals in Engineering and Technology. The College of Engineering for Women is sponsored by 'Gayatri Vidya Parishad' Society which was established about two and a half decades back. The Parishad is proud to have renowned erstwhile Vice-chancellors, academicians, Engineers, Legal luminaries, Industrialists, Entrepreneurs, philanthropists and elite of Visakhapatnam on its Governing Body. The College of Engineering for Women has very good infrastructural facilities, e-class rooms, well equipped laboratories, well qualified faculty to train the youngsters who are in pursuit of their professional careers. Present day demands of the youth in their academic arena will be met in the well planned academic and personality development programs organized by the College for the benefit of the students. I greet all those who had this College as their choice to pursue their professional careers. I wish them a successful professionalism for the overall welfare of the individual in particular and society at large.
